Why Choose Us?

The Business Cafe is currently making good coffee for the local people, and preparing to bring the more people into cafe. Our community is thriving and growing every day. Please become a member, find us on Twitter and be sure to join our Facebook group too.

The Café will deliver free membership and free digital skills via a network of high street coffee shops for sole-traders, SMEs and business people.

As well as a beautiful private members lounge-style, environment serving high quality food and beverages, members will enjoy a culture of peer support and community and will have access to free ad hoc support with the digital applications they use in their business.
